PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: 12 December 2017

Cyber Credit is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy tells you about our online collection and use of data. The terms of this policy apply to all Cyber Credit websites, including our global sites, unless different terms are specified in a form or contract provided to you online or offline.

For data protection purposes, Cyber Credit is the controller and is also the processor of data. Information collected may be retained, and may be stored, processed, accessed, and used in jurisdictions whose privacy laws may be different and less protective than those of your home jurisdiction. Most of the data collecting process happens during registration. Some is required and some is optional, some will be displayed to other users while some will remain private. At some points, you might be required to submit your identification and/or your passport as a part of Know Your Customer (KYC) act. By visiting Cyber Credit websites, you agree and consent to of these terms described in this privacy policy.

Personal Information Collecting

Cyber Credit collects certain kinds of information, such as the website that referred you to us, your IP address, browser type and language, and access times. We also may collect navigational information, including information about the pages you view, the links you click, and other actions taken in connection with the site.

We may combine personal information that you provide and other public information for verifying purposes. You may always choose not to provide such information, but if you so choose, certain modules and services may not be available to you. Personal information (e.g., full name, email address, password, mobile number, date of birth, your mailing address) is collected when you register. Additional personal information (e.g., your government ID number and/or passport number, including your face holding your IDs) might be required to process further transactions or to serve you with advanced products and services.

Personal Information Uses

Cyber Credit collects and uses your personal information to operate and improve our sites, to process your transactions, to provide customer service, to perform research and analysis aimed at improving our products, services and technologies, and to display content that is customized to your interests and preferences.

The personal information can be employed for communicating purposes. We may send service and promotion-related communications such as welcome letters, profit reminders, and lending confirmations. We may also send you surveys or marketing emails to inform you of new interests or change of services or other information that may be of interest.

Personal Information Sharing

Except as disclosed in this privacy policy, Cyber Credit does not share your personal information with any third parties.

Within our corporation:

We may share your personal information within our corporate group, including with our subsidiaries and divisions, all of whom may use your information for the purposes disclosed herein.

With our partners:

We may share your personal information with service providers who perform services on our behalf. For example, we may hire other companies to verify KYC progress, to direct/indirect marketing, or to conduct audits, etc. Those companies will be permitted to obtain only a limited personal information they need to provide the service. They are required to maintain the confidentiality of the information and are prohibited from using it for any other purpose.

With other users:

You and other users can choose which information to share, with the oriented audiences or public. For example, you want to show your profit to promote for your work, or to advocate your believes.

Social Media and Trusted Third Parties:

On some pages, we allow you to share personal information with third parties, such as social networks like Facebook. In these instances, you are agreeing to the data being shared and the shared data is subject to the privacy policies of the third parties. We do not control and do not assume any responsibility for the use of personal information by such third parties. For more information about the third party’s purpose and scope of their use of personal information in connection with sharing features, please visit the privacy policies of such third parties.

Bitcoin, Ethereum and others blockchains:

A limited information can be leaked via the blockchains. Please follow the guides provided by according websites for further information. For privacy purposes, members remain anonymous on the sites and are identified only by their chosen screen names. The lending and trading programs, and token and coin implements have to provide an open Application Program Interface (API) to Coinmarketcap, Cryptocompare, and Worldcoinindex. Please visit such websites for more details.

Personal Information Accessing:

We will not sell, distribute, nor lease your personal information to third parties unless we have your explicit permission or are required by law to do so in the case of a legal investigation.

Third Party Advertising and Traffic Analysing

We also use third party service providers to optimize advertisements (banners or links) and traffics on our behalf across the internet. These service-providers, such as Google Analytics and Alexa Traffic Analyser may collect information about your visits to our website and your interactions with our products and services as well as your visits to other websites. In many cases, this information could be useful and be employed for your interests by our trusted partners.
